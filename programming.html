Cyber Intelligence

Cyber Intelligence is a game I made for Cyberpunk Jam 007 on itch.io

It's a short game about being a stealthy assassin and taking out guards to retrieve data from a computer terminal

Due to time constraints and me developing solo, the game is a bit buggy. Especially the enemy behavior is very jank. It was my first time experimenting with NPC behavior and using

You can play it here if you'd like

All level design, programming and music was done by me on this project. I didn't have enough time to make my own props so most of the visual assets are not created by me

Doobobulatron

Doobobulatron is a game me and my team of 6 worked on for the GDK Space jam 25

It's a puzzle game about being on a decaying space ship that you need to save. The way tos save the ship is activating different terminals around the ship, while avoiding things that come in your way.

I did all level design and some scripting for a portion where you have to avoid live wires or else they will kill you.
