Cyber Intelligence

Cyber Intelligence is a game I made for Cyberpunk Jam 007 on itch.io

It's a short game about being a stealthy assassin and taking out guards to retrieve data from a computer terminal

It was made solo in two weeks in the godot engine

You can play it here if you'd like

All level design, programming and music was done by me on this project. I didn't have enough time to make my own props so most of the visual assets are not created by me
